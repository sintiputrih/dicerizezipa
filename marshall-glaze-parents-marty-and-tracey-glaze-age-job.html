Who Are Marshall Glaze Parents? Meet Marty And Tracey Glaze!

Marshall Glaze is one of the contestants in Season 4 of Netflix’s Love Is Blind. In case you missed it, he is also a fan-favorite. And fans have not only been curious about him, but they have also wanted to know about his parents Marty and Tracy Glaze. So, the writing below is just about them.

Love Is Blind Who Are Marshall Glaze’s Parents?

Marshall Glaze’s parents are seemingly lovely, supportive folks. At the same time, they seem very much fond of Marshall, and he of them. Let us get to know each one of them, together and individually in the rest of the writing.

Meet Marty Glaze, Marshall Glaze’s Father

Marshall Glaze’s father, Marty Glaze is the son of the late Hilah Theresa (Brown) Glaze and Jack of Baltimore, Maryland. The now-deceased couple had the privilege of renewing their wedding vows on their 50th wedding anniversary in August 2001, just two weeks before Jack was called home to heaven. Hilah followed him later in August 2010.

Marty also grew up alongside his siblings Jackie, Renee, Mark, and Lisa.

Marty’s mom, who had a tremendous love for family, instilled that value in her kids as well. And together, she and her husband also made sure that their children know the value of a good education. As a result, all of them including Marty successfully earned college degrees.

  • Marty Glaze Age

Marty Glaze was born around the last week of February. What day and what year exactly was not known though.

  • Marty Glaze Job

Since 2003 and as of March of 2023, Marty Glaze has been working as the President of Commercial Construction in Hanover, Maryland.

On his LinkedIn, Marty also gushed about having over 30 years of experience in the construction industry and having worked for some of the largest firms in Maryland. All these times, he said, his mission has been to create the best and finest general contracting company that can compete at the highest levels.

He acquires a B.S. in Civil Engineering from Union College. He went there for the program from 1982 – 1986.

  • Is Marty Glaze On Instagram?

Yes. Marty Glaze could be found on Instagram @mdglaze with 144 posts and 308 followers as of 2 April 2023.

Meet Tracey Glaze, Marshall Glaze’s Mother

Marshall Glaze’s mother Tracey Glaze is one of nine children born of the late Nelson Mc Millan, Sr., and the late Ollie Mae McMillan. The fourth one of them, her sister the late Eunice Marie Hunter of Glen Rogers, West Virginia passed away on 31 March 2020.

The remaining siblings of Tracey are her three brothers, Matt McMillan (married to Gwen), Ray McMillan, and Lorenzo McMillan (married to Alice), and two sisters, Lillie Hatcher, and Penny McMillan. Their oldest brother, Nelson Mc Millan, Jr., and sister Margo McMillan are no more.

  • Tracey Glaze Age

Tracey Glaze was born in March 1964. So, she turned 59 years old in 2023.

  • Tracey Glaze Job

Professionally, Tracey Glaze is a registered nurse working in Baltimore City County, Maryland. On her LinkedIn, she talked about her 20+ years of experience as a registered nurse with an extensive background in multiple acute care settings.

Since March 2016 and as of now, she has been working as a travel registered nurse around Boca Raton, Florida. Currently, she seemed to be working as a float nurse at Northwest Hospital in Baltimore. She has also for 8 different hospitals in the Maryland and Virginia areas.

She acquires a bachelor’s degree in Registered Nursing from the University of Maryland Baltimore.

  • Is Tracey Glaze On Instagram?

Tracey Glaze does not seem to have any social media accounts, including an Instagram one, as of April 2023.
