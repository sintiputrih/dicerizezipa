Karis has been a fashion/beauty writer with over 6 years of experience. She started with a lifestyle blog and it all grew from there. Read more at: Everything24Karis.com.

Experience

Karis has been a fashion/beauty writer for over 6 years, starting with a lifestyle blog Everything24Karis.com discussing fashion, beauty and DIYs and then launching her own magazine FLAME where she is the Editor-in-Chief. Karis is also a published stylist, costume designer and personal shopper. With 10+ years in styling editorial, lifestyle and commercial shoots and wardrobe for Film/TV. In addition to writing, Karis also attends fashion weeks for event and show coverage and interviewing front row guests, designers and backstage.

Education

Karis graduated with a AA in Fashion Marketing from The Art Institute and a MA in Fashion Journalism from Academy of Art University.
