Chez Reavie Biography – Chez Reavie Wiki & Wikipedia

Chez Reavie full name William Chesney Reavie, is an American professional golfer.

Chez graduated from Dobson High School in May of 2000. He graduated from Arizona State University in December of 2010 with a B.I.S. degree, with concentrations in political science and sociology.

Chez played college golf at Arizona State University. He played on the Nationwide Tour from 2005 through 2007, where he won once at the 2007 Knoxville Open. He finished 18th on the money list on the Nationwide Tour in 2007 to earn his PGA Tour card.

Chez’s rookie season on the PGA Tour was in 2008, during which he won his maiden PGA Tour title, at the 2008 RBC Canadian Open on July 27, 2008. On June 23, 2019, Reavie captured his second PGA Tour win at the Travelers Championship.
