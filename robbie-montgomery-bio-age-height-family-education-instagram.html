Robbie Montgomery Bio, Age, Height, Family, Education, Instagram

Robbie Montgomery is a singer and entrepreneur, known for being a part of the original Ikettes in the Ike & Tina Turner Revue as far back as the 1960s.

Born on June 16, 1940, in Columbus, Mississippi, Robbie toured Europe in the 1980s with former members of the Kings of Rhythm, including Jimmy Hinds, Clayton Love, Billy Gayles, Erskine Oglesby, Stacy Johnson and Oliver Sain.

Robbie was a member of the Mirettes, and later a “Night Tripper” for Dr. John.

She served as back up singer for the likes of Stevie Wonder, Barbra Streisand, the Rolling Stones, and Joe Cocker.
