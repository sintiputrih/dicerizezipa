Real World Bad Boy Adam Royer Seeks Redemption on MTV Spin-Off

He's baaack!

One month after being booted from the Real World: Las Vegas cast for excessive drinking and violent partying, MTV bad boy Adam Royer is ready for more.

The 22-year-old is set to take part in MTV's The Challenge: Rivals starting June 22 and Us Weekly has a look at the show's explosive new trailer.

Joining Royer for the competition series — formerly known as Real World/Road Rules Challenge — will be 28 fan favorite contestants including Challenge vets Paula (Real World: Key West), Wes (Real World: Austin) and CT (Real World: Paris). In a new twist this season, the contestants will be paired up with their sworn enemies in an attempt to win $300,000.

Royer is ready and waiting for his competition when the battle begins in Costa Rica.

"I'm fearless. I'll eat anything. I'll jump off of anything. I'll do whatever," Royer tells MTV. "I don't care if I get hurt or what happens."

Get a sneak peek of The Challenge: Rivals drama in the clip above ahead of the show's premiere June 22 at 10 p.m. (EST) on MTV.
