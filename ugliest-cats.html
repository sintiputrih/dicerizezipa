5 Of The Weirdest And Ugliest Cat Breeds From Around The World

Devon Rex

Hailing from England, the Devon Rex cat makes up for its rather unsightly appearance with its sharp intelligence. Most famously dubbed as a monkey in a cat’s suit, the Devon Rex is capable of learning how to fetch, jump and tag with much more sophistication than your average Labrador.
