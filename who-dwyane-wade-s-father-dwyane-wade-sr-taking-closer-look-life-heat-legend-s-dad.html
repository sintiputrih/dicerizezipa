Who is Dwyane Wade's father Dwyane Wade Sr.? Taking closer look at life of Heatlegend'sdad

Dwyane Wade's induction into the Hall of Fame marked his well-deserved recognition as one of basketball's greatest players. Clad in a sharp white suit, he delivered an exceptional tribute to the individual he referred to as the "most important person" in his life.

Dwyane Wade brought his dad onto the dais and gave "pops" his flowers, recollecting important moments in his journey to the top. There was no dry eye by the time the applause died down.

Going toe to toe with his son for being the best dressed in the room, Wade Sr. was in a sharp bottle green suit and looked like he was more of an elder brother than a father to the latest entrant into the HoF.

What impact has Dwyane Wade Sr. had on the Hall of Famer's career?

Wade Sr. took custody of him at the tender age of nine as his mother, JoLinda, struggled with drug problems. Wade spent his formative years under the watchful eye of his father and stepmother. He moved away from a life of guns and violence, into one of rigor, discipline, and basketball.
