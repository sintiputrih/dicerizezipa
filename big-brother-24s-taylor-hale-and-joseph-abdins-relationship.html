The Truth About Big Brother 24's Taylor Hale and Joseph Abdin's Relationship

Taylor Hale took the Big Brother house by storm this summer. Watching Taylor emerge from her underdog status to win the game and become the first winner to be crowned America's Favorite Player was magical. But viewers also witnessed another type of magic between Taylor and housemate Joseph Abdin.Taylor and Joseph bonded almost immediately in the house. The pair were incredibly close throughout their time together in the house, but when Joseph was blindsided and evicted, fans feared the duo would lose their spark from not being together. But as soon as the two were reunited, the sparks began flying again. Now that the cameras aren't tracking their every move, fans want to know what's going on between Taylor and Joseph. Are the pair just close friends, or have the self-proclaimed soulmates finally put a label on it?

Have Taylor And Joseph Been Hanging Out After Leaving The Big Brother House?

Taylor and Joseph have been inseparable since leaving the Big Brother House. While at first, the two spent time with their other house guests in Los Angeles, soon, the pair started spending more one-on-one time together. Joseph has had the opportunity to get to know what Taylor is like in real life, and vice versa.

They began hosting fan events together, posting short videos of their time together and even dressed up in Halloween costumes that were an homage to their shared veto punishment.

The pair spent two weeks together before heading home to visit their families. It was an excellent time for them to get to know one another. While they learned a lot about each other in the Big Brother house, the real world has been a more substantial test of their relationship. They want to be one of the Big Brother showmances that stands the test of time.

Joseph has said that his family loves Taylor and always asks about her. They've become very protective of her, which Joseph says warms his heart. Taylor was able to introduce Joseph to her mother at the Miss USA pageant they all went to in October 2022.

Did Taylor Bring Joseph On The Cruise To Mexico?

When things got romantic between Taylor and Monte, fans were worried that the new couple throttled all chances of a Taylor and Joseph Reunion - let alone their vacation to Mexico. Taylor won one of the coveted vacation prizes during a Veto competition in the Big Brother House this summer. After doing so, she promised her housemate Joseph that she would be taking him as her plus one.

But fans can rest easy knowing that when the cruise left, both Taylor and Joseph were present. After returning, Taylor chatted with E! News about the trip. When they asked why she brought Joseph, the Big Brother 24 winner said, "It only felt right to me that I could have someone who felt like my right-hand man throughout the game," Taylor said.

"Anybody who watched the season knew Joseph and I were on the same wavelength, and I have pulled apart from him for a long time. It felt like a really good opportunity to have some quality time," said the winner.

However, the pair did learn some things they hadn't known about each other while they were in Mexico. Joseph joked that one of the growing pains between the two is that Taylor is very much a morning person - even on vacation.

Are Joseph And Taylor Dating?

Fans are dying to know if Joseph and Taylor have made it official since leaving the Big Brother house. However, the reality stars weren't in a rush to put a label on things. Taylor has repeatedly spoken about why she and Joseph were okay with waiting. They both want to ensure their friendship is deep enough before delving into a romantic relationship. During an Instagram live in early November, the Big Brother winner told fans that the two needed to have some serious conversations before making things official.

Those conversations must have happened during the pair's trip to Mexico, as Taylor and Joseph confirmed to People Magazine on November 9th that they are officially dating. Taylor told the magazine that ever since Joseph kissed her on finale night, she knew the two would find a way to be together. "His heart is bigger than the galaxy, and now we have the privilege of sharing our hearts with each other. I love him so much - I'm the happiest I've ever been," said Taylor

"She is a remarkable woman that I am so grateful to call mine. She may have won Big Brother, but I won so much more," said the lawyer about his girlfriend. Joseph added that while their journey to being in a relationship has been a rollercoaster of an experience, he wouldn't want to share it with anyone else.
