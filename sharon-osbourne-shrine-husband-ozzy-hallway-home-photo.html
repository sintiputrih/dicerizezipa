Sharon Osbourne astounds fans with shrine to husband Ozzy at grand home

The presenter's home looks incredible

    star Sharon Osbourne has shared a glimpse into her private home where there's a hallway dedicated to her husband Ozzy Osbourne, who is living with Parkinson's disease. 

    The shrine-like display features many of Ozzy's framed records and pictures of the rock icon. Above the tributes, there is also a massive stained glass window of Jesus.
