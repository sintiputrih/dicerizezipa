Daniel Jones height and weight Measurement in meters, feet, KG and Ibs

Daniel Stephen Jones III is an American, who plays football quarterback for the New York Giants of the National Football League. Jones was born on March 27, 1997, in Charlotte, North Carolina.

He began his football career at Charlotte Latin School in Charlotte, North Carolina, during which he had 6,997 passing yards and 98 touchdowns, however, he was barely recruited after high school and was not ranked by Rivals.com.

Despite that, he was originally committed to Princeton University for a collegiate career but then opted to become a walk-on at Duke University. He played college football at Duke and was selected sixth overall by the Giants in the 2019 NFL Draft.

As football lovers wish to know every detail about their loved and cheered players, questions were raised about the body measurement of Daniel Jones. In that regard, we discuss below his height and weight.

Height in meters and feet

Jones can be considered a tall sports personality by his height. He stands at a height of 6 feet and 5 inches, which when converted into meters, will be 1.95, or 195 centimeters.

Weight in KG and lbs

With his height mentioned above, Jones has a proportional body mass. He weighs 104 kg, which is equivalent to 230 lbs. Though his weight might change, we updated you with the most recently checked.
