Bobby Cannavale Doesn't Get Why People Care If He &amp; Partner Rose Byrne Are Married Or Not

Just a few weeks after Rose Byrne opened up about why she and partner Bobby Cannavale aren’t married, he’s now speaking out too.

In a recent interview with InStyle magazine, the 51-year-old Nine Perfect Strangers star doesn’t get why people are that interested in their martial status.

Click inside to read more…

“I don’t know,” he answered on when he and Rose would tie the knot, and added that “I don’t know why people care. Why do people give a sh**?”

Bobby went on, saying that “the only people that should care are her parents and my parents. You know what I mean? They don’t care, so why would anyone else care?”

Previously, Rose revealed that they’re just not in a rush to walk down the aisle at all.

“I love weddings, and I know people [for whom] it’s an important thing, and I respect that totally. I guess for us it’s just been, we didn’t do it, we’ll do it, then – no! Pandemic,” she shared.

Rose and Bobby share two sons, Rocco, 5, and Rafa, 3.
