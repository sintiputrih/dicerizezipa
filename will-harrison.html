Meet Will Harrison, Who Played Will Harrison in “Daisy Jones & The Six”

Will Harrison (born on August 21, 1996) is a 26-year-old American actor who gained fame after landing the part of Graham Dunne in Scott Neustadter’s TV series “Daisy Jones & The Six” which aired on Netflix on March 3, 2023.

It is a musical drama which is based on Taylor Jenkins Reid’s hit novel, which covers the story of the rise and fall of the famous 70s band Fleetwood Mac. The lead roles in this James Ponsoldt-directed series are played by exceptional actors like Riley Keough, Sam Claflin, Camila Morrone, and Suki Waterhouse.

This is not the first time Will starred in a TV production; previously, he appeared in season 6 of “Madam Secretary” alongside Téa Leoni, who played the role of the main character Elizabeth McCord. He was also in Tess Harrison-directed film “This Is a Film About My Mother,” which landed on the theatre screens on December 31, 2022.

How Old is Will Harrison?

On August 21, 1996, Will Harrison (age 26) was born to his father, John Harrison, and mother, Janine Harrison, in Ithaca, New York State, America. His sister, Tess Harrison, is also an actress, who you might have seen playing Eve in “This Is a Film About My Mother.” According to CHQ Daily, Will grew up in Hadley, Massachusetts, and spent his whole childhood there.

In an interview, he accepted his interest in acting peaked because of movies; as a kid, he was very much into action movies and loved recreating scenes. His love for acting connected him to a local theatre, and he worked as a stage artist for years.

After high school, he joined the Carnegie Mellon School of Drama and improved his acting skills. He also worked with the Chautauqua Theater Company for a long time.

Will Harrison’s Acting Career

Harrison was only 19 when he got to work on a short movie titled “Dog,” and it was his first project in front of a camera. The same year he worked on one more project, but this time, he got to work for and with his sister Tess. Through “It’s Perfect Here,” the Harrison siblings displayed the bond between a brother and a sister.

In 2018, Will auditioned for the high-rated CBS thriller “Madam Secretary.” Fortunately, Mark Saks liked his performances and selected him for the role of Zach Barnow. Meanwhile, the young worked on two more short films: “In Arms” and “Venefica.”

In 2023, this NY native’s popularity skyrocketed after he joined the cast of “Daisy Jones & The Six.” Viewers loved how well he portrayed Zach Barnow despite being his only second major project.
