Ginnifer Goodwin, Josh Dallas Married, Visit Friend After Wedding

The fairy tale continues! Newlywed couple Ginnifer Goodwin and Josh Dallas stopped by a friend’s house in L.A. on Sunday, April 13, the day after they exchanged vows in an intimate wedding ceremony.

Goodwin, 35, pregnant with the couple’s first child, gave photographers a smile as she stepped out of the car. The actress, who plays Snow White on Once Upon a Time opposite her husband’s role as Prince Charming, covered her burgeoning baby bump with a light spring coat. Dallas, 32, looked handsome in a dark coat and shades.

According to the photographer, the Killing Kennedy actress showed off her diamond ring and baby bump before she walked into the home. Goodwin’s rep confirmed to Us Weekly that the couple exchanged vows on Saturday, April 12. For her wedding, the Something Borrowed star wore a custom-designed Monique Lhuillier gown.
