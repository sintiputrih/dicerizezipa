Zayn Malik’s Net Worth

Zayn Malik | Music, Endorsements & Books

Music

After One Direction dissolved in 2015, Malik embarked upon a solo music career. Zayn signed a recording deal with RCA records.

He released his first studio album entitled ‘Mind of Mine‘ in 2016, adopting a more alternative R&B music style.

The lead single ‘Pillowtalk’ made Malik the first British male artist to debut at number one in both the United States and the United Kingdom. As of this writing, the single has over 1 billion views on YouTube.

Similarly, it also tossed Malik to No. 6 on the Billboard Artist 100 chart.

Also, ‘Like I Would,’ the second single off the album, reached No. 1 on the Billboard Dance Club Songs chart.

Moreover, at the end of 2016, Zayn collaborated with Taylor Swift for the next project entitled ‘I Don’t Wanna Live Forever,’ an official soundtrack of the film “Fifty Shades Darker.”

The track hit No. 5 in the UK, No. 1 in Sweden, and No. 2 in America.

To date, Zayn has released three studio albums, 16 music videos, six promotional singles, and 17 singles.

His most recent project is ‘Nobody is Listening,’ released in 2021 under RCA Records.

Endorsements

Zayn Malik has been targeted by several fashion houses to come up with a line of fashion accessories for both males and females.

He made his debut on The Business of Fashion’s annual BoF500 index in 2016. The index profiles the most influential people in fashion.

He collaborated with Italian footwear designer Giuseppe Zanotti in January of 2017 to release a shoe line.

The shoe line features several shoe styles, including two sneakers and two boots.

Similarly, Versace released a campaign in February 2017 featuring model Adwoah Aboah and Zayn Malik for their Spring Summer collection.

Moreover, Zayn was appointed by Versace’s chief designer Donatella Versace herself as the creative director for women’s and men’s capsule collections called Zayn x Versus.

Furthermore, Zayn designed a backpack for The Kooples in September 2018.

He was featured in the Spring 2018 and 2019 campaign of Penshoppe.

In addition to that, he modeled for Martyre, a lifestyle and accessory brand founded by Malik’s ex-girlfriend Gigi Hadid’s brother Anwar Hadid.

Book Publications

Zayn Malik is also a published author.

It contains Zayn’s never-before-seen pictures alongside his story.
