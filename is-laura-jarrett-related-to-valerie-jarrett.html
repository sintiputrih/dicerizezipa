Is Laura Jarrett Related To Valerie Jarrett?

Yes, they are related. Laura Jarrett is the daughter of Valerie Jarrett. The mother-daughter relationship has gone through several major changes throughout their lifetime.

When asked how she felt growing up in her mother’s shadow, Laura said she never felt like she was in her shadow. “But that’s more a testament to her and her great work, ” the journalist added.

The young reporter disclosed that she accompanied her mom while she worked in city government. Whenever people would yell and be aggressive toward Valerie, Laura would be horrified, thinking this was what she had to deal with in her everyday job.

“It was good for me to see her everyday life,” said NBC News official stated. According to Laura, her mother never projected anything onto her and never brought baggage home.

On the other hand, the former government official remarked “mother” is the most important position she will ever hold as it is only one which is permanent.

Valerie thinks that she is far from the perfect mom. She didn’t have time to cook or bake, so if there were a potluck, she’d be the one bringing the paper plates and napkins.

“If Laura didn’t value her attendance at events, such as school open houses, she would skip them,” added Valerie.

“We managed to do the things that were important to her or that I knew would be significant to me even though I didn’t always have much time with her,” said the Chief Executive Officer of the Obama Foundation.
