MEET The Family Of Nollywood Actor Bob Manuel Odokwu

Bob-Manuel Obidimma Udokwu is a Nigerian actor, director, producer, and politician. He is one of the pioneers and legends the movie industry has produced.

Bob Manuel has received several awards since he started his acting career. In 2014, he received the Lifetime Achievement award at the 10th Africa Movie Academy Awards. He also got a nomination for Best Actor in a supporting role at the 2013 Nollywood Movies Awards for his role in Adesuwa.
