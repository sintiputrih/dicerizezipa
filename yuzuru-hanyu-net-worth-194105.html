Yuzuru Hanyu Net Worth

Yuzuru Hanyu is a Japanese figure skater who was born on December 7, 1994. He is a two-time Olympic gold medalist in the men's singles event, having won gold at the 2014 and 2018 Olympics. He also won first-place in the same event at the 2014 and 2017 World Championships, making him the first Asian figure skater competing in men's singles to win the Olympic gold.
