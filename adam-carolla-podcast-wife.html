Know About Adam Carolla; Podcast, Net Worth, Wife, Show, Height

Published Date: 22 May, 2019 Updated date: 22 May, 2019 By Celebs Podium

During the verge of their emerging career, Adam Carolla met his future wife and dated for six years before their nuptials take place. It has been more than a decade of their marriage life and couple made their family of four with the arrival of twins.

American comedian Adam Carolla is a prominent name in the media field. The multitalented personality hosts The Adam Carolla Show, a free podcast which set the Guinness World Records as the “most downloaded podcast” in 2011.

Adam Carolla Bio; Parents, Education, Height

Adam was born on 27 May 1964 to parents Jim Carolla and Kris McCall in Los Angeles County, California. His parents were separated when he was young and grew up with his sister in the North Hollywood neighborhood of Los Angeles.

Standing at the height of 6 feet 2 inches (1.88 meters), attended Colfax Elementary School, Walter Reed Junior High, and North Hollywood High School. Afterward, he briefly attended Los Angeles Valley College but dropped out in search of jobs.

His Career; Podcast, Show

It the beginning, Carolla was a co-host of the syndicated radio call-in program Loveline from 1995 to 2005. Similarly, he co-hosted and co-created the television program, The Man Show on Comedy Central from 1999 to 2004.

In 2009, he started a daily podcast on his personal website, which later evolved into the ACE Broadcasting Network. Likewise, in 2011, he claimed on Jimmy Kimmel Live! that The Adam Carolla Show had taken the Guinness World Record for the most downloaded podcast. It received 59,574,843 unique downloads from 2009 to 2011.

Adam Carolla hosts the podcast, The Adam Carolla Show since 2009 (Picture: AXS)

Apart from the podcast and shows, Adam appeared in numerous movie roles. His acting credits include The Hammer (2007), Winning: The Racing Life of Paul Newman (2015), and The 24 Hour War (2016).

Net Worth In Millions, House, Cars

When it comes to earning, Adam bags the prominent part of net worth from his comedy stint. The veteran comedian currently enjoys a whopping net worth of an estimated $16 million.

Moreover, he owns a $7.327 million contemporary-style house located in Los Angeles. Previously, he listed his mid-century modern home located in the La Canada Flintridge neighborhood of Los Angeles on sale for $3.4 million. Besides, he is a passionate automobile collector with over 20 cars that includes several Lamborghinis from the 1960s and early 1970s.

Father Of Twin Children From Wife

Adam, aged 54 has been enjoying the marital relationship with the love of his life for more than a decade. He married Lynette Paradise on 28 September 2002.

The couple first met during the early phase of their career and dated for six years prior to their wedding. Lynette is the co-host of For CryingOut Loud on PodcastOne. Moreover, she is the CEO and Brand Ambassador of Carolla Drinks, which includes Endless Rant IPA and Lager.

Adam Carolla poses with his wife Lynette Paradise and their twins on the occasion of the New Year 2019 (Picture: Lynette’s Instagram)

Adam and Lynette bear two children from their relationship. The pair welcomed twins; a son named Santino “Sonny” Richard Carolla and a daughter named Natalia Carolla on 7 June 2006.

Some Interesting Facts About Adam Carolla

  • Adam’s parents did not give him a middle name. Meanwhile, he listed his middle name as “Lakers” as a joke on his driver’s license application, which was processed without notice.
  • In his early years, he worked as a carpet cleaner, carpenter, boxing instructor, and traffic school instructor.
  • He was co-owner of an Italian restaurant, Amalfi in Los Angeles, which is closed now.
  • He is an author of books that include Not Taco Bell Material Not Taco Bell Material (2012), President Me: The America That’s in My Head (2014), and Daddy, Stop Talking! (2015).
