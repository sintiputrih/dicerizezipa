Cannon was born on the 1st of June in 1989. Therefore she is currently 31 years old. Moreover, her zodiac sign is Gemini.

Emma Cannon Career at College:

During Cannon’s freshman year itself, she was named to the C-USA All-Freshman team. In addition, Emma Cannon continues to keep up her impressive records . Emma continued to keep up her individual and team success.  She was also named an NCAA Division-II All-American Honorable Mention.

Emma Cannon WNBA Career:

Cannon had to wait until 2017 before making her way into the league. In the year 2017, Cannon signed with the Phoenix Mercury and appeared in 34 games. Emma finished that season averaging 4.4 points per game and 3.6 rebounds per game. In addition, she led all WNBA rookies in field goal percentage. After that, Cannon signed again with Phoenix in 2018 in hopes once again to make the roster. However, she was cut in training camp.

After that, Emma signed a training camp contract with the Connecticut Sun. This was in the year 2019. However, once again didn’t make the final squad and was cut before the season began.

Following that Emma Cannon signed with the Las Vegas Aces on September 9 . She was eligible to play on the 12th after completing her quarantine period due to the COVID-19 protocols of the WNBA Bubble. In addition, Emma Cannon signed again with them in 2021 and made the Opening Day roster for the Aces. She played in 3 games in 2021 before being waived on May 31st, 2021.

After being waived by Vegas, she signed with the Connecticut on June 7, 2021. Here, She signed as a replacement/hardship player after Jonquel Jones left due to an overseas commitment.

After she was released by the Sun she signed a7-day contract with Indiana Fever.
