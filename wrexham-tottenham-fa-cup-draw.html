Wrexham to host Tottenham in FA Cup fifth round if they can beat Sheffield United

National League side Wrexham will host Tottenham Hotspur in the fifth round of the FA Cup if they beat Sheffield United in a replay.

Wrexham, who are owned by Hollywood actors Ryan Reynolds and Rob McElhenney, played out a pulsating 3-3 draw against Sheffield United on Sunday.

The fifth tier side looked to be heading to the fifth round of England’s famous cup competition before John Egan made it 3-3 in second-half stoppage time.

Advertisement

Wrexham will travel to Championship side Sheffield United on February 7 when the two sides will battle it out to take on Tottenham, who are fifth in the Premier League.

Elsewhere, Southampton manager Nathan Jones will return to Luton Town if his former side beat Grimsby Town in their fourth-round replay.

Manchester United will host either Derby County or West Ham United, who face off on Monday night, while Manchester City will travel to Bristol City.

Brighton & Hove Albion will face Championship side Stoke City after knocking out holders Liverpool thanks to Kaoru Mitoma’s late winner.

FA Cup fifth round draw in full 

Southampton v Luton or Grimsby

Leicester v Blackburn or Birmingham

Stoke v Brighton

Wrexham or Sheffield United v Tottenham

Fulham or Sunderland v Leeds

Bristol City v Man City
