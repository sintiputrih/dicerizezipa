About

NBA point guard best known for his seven seasons leading the Sacramento Kings from 2001 to 2008. After his time in Sacramento, he played for the Atlanta Hawks, Washington Wizards and Miami Heat before finishing his career with the New York Knicks in 2012.

Before Fame

He won the NCAA title as a freshmen with the Arizona Wildcats under head coach Lute Olson.
