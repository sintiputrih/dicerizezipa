Nikkita Lyons Breaks Her Silence After Shocking Betrayal By Zoey Stark On WWE NXT

Nikkita Lyons was struck by surprise when her partner Zoey Stark knocked her down in the ring. Things fell apart on NXT after the two failed to capture the Women’s Tag Team Titles. In response to this shocking development, Lyons has finally spoken out.

For some time now, Nikkita Lyons and Zoey Stark have been working together. They attempted to win the NXT Women’s Tag Team Championships against Katana Chance and Kayden Carter, but they were unsuccessful. Zoey later knocked out her partner with one of those titles to make matters worse.

Following Stark’s unexpected heel turn, Lyons eventually broke her silence on Twitter. She retweeted the tag team match where everything happened while using the broken heart emoji. She is obviously devastated by Zoey’s abrupt change of heart.

Lyons and Stark had previously failed to defeat the reigning champions, Katana Chance and Kayden Carter. They have consistently failed to defeat the reigning champion team. Lyons and Stark may get into a fight now that they are both planning to concentrate once more on their individual careers.

Nikkita Lyons has once again captured the WWE Universe’s attention. This might be the crucial step toward her joining WWE’s main roster. Only time will tell how this rivalry unfolds.
