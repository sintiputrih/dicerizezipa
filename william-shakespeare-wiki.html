Who Was William Shakespeare? - William Shakespeare Wikipedia

William Shakespeare Wiki:- William Shakespeare was born on 26 April 1564 in Stratford-upon-Avon, England. He died on 23 April 1616 in Stratford-upon-Avon, England. William Shakespeare was a writer, poet, and actor from England. He is largely considered the finest English-language writer and the world's preeminent playwright. He is known as the "Bard of Avon" (or simply "the Bard") and England's national poet. His existing works, including collaborations, include 39 plays, 154 sonnets, three big narrative poems, and a few additional verses, the authorship of which is unknown.

 His plays have been translated into every major living language and have had more productions than any other dramatist. During Shakespeare's lifetime, many of his plays were published in versions of variable quality and authenticity. However, in 1623, two of Shakespeare's fellow players and colleagues, John Heminges and Henry Condell, released the First Folio, a posthumous collected edition of Shakespeare's dramatic works that included 36 of his plays. Its Preface was a foresighted poem by Ben Jonson, a former adversary of Shakespeare, in which he praised Shakespeare with the now-famous term "not of an age, but for all time."
