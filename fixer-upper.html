Fixer Upper News - Us Weekly

Network:HGTV

The reality series Fixer Upper stars married couple Chip and Joanna Gaines, who own a home renovation and design business in Waco, Texas. The twosome work with clients to buy and remodel homes on camera.

Fixer Upper premiered in 2013 and ran for five seasons until 2018. In 2020, it was announced that the series would be revived for Magnolia Network, a sister channel to HGTV overseen by the Gaineses. There have been three installations since: Fixer Upper: Welcome Home in 2021, Fixer Upper: The Castle in 2022 and Fixer Upper: The Hotel in 2023.
