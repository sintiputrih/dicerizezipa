Who is Zahi Hawass:

Zahi Hawass is a famous Archeologist. He was born on May 28, 1947 and his birthplace is Egypt. Zahi is also well known as, Egyptian archaeologist who served as his country’s Minister of State for Antiquities Affairs in 2011. He’s worked at numerous archaeological sites including the Nile Delta, the Western Desert and the Upper Nile Valley.

Zahi is originated from Egypt. He held a prominent role in the administration of former president Hosni Mubarak.
