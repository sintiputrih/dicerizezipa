Who is Cindy Crawford?

Cindy Crawford whose full name is Cynthia Ann Crawford was born on February 20, 1966, in DeKalb, Illinois in the United States of America. She grew up with her mother Jennifer, a bank teller, father Dan, an electrician and sisters Chris and Danielle. Cindy also had a brother, Jeffrey, who sadly passed away from childhood leukemia when he was just 3 years old.Crawford attended DeKalb High School and took part in a fashion shoot for a local store during her junior year. Later that year, photographer Roger Legel took Cindy’s photo for “DeKalb Nite Weekly,” and at age 17, she was runner-up in the Elite Model Management’s Look of the Year contest. Crawford was the valedictorian of her high school when she graduated in 1984, and she briefly attended Northwestern University on a chemical engineering scholarship.She dropped out of college to focus on modeling, and after she moved to New York, she was signed by the Elite New York modeling agency in 1986.
