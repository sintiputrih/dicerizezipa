Kourtney Kardashian's Son Mason Dash Disick Massive Net Worth Despite Tender Age

Mason Disick is a child American reality star, who started starring in The Keeping Up With The Kardashians before his birth. He was born in the Season 4 finale of the Keeping Up With The Kardashians.

In recent times, Mason Dash Disick has made media headlines for his appearances in TikTok videos with his mother, Kourtney Kardashian, and the Tik Tok star, Addison Rae as well.

Mason Dash Disick's Family

Widely known as the son of Kourtney Kardashian and her ex-beau, Scott Disick, he is the nephew of Kim Kardashian, Khloé Kardashian & Rob Kardashian. Similarly, he is a half-nephew of Kendall Jenner & Kylie Jenner. He grew up with his siblings, namely, Penelope Scotland Disick and Reign Aston Disick.

Mason Dash Disick with the Kardashian family (©: Zimbio)

Mason Dash Disick is the grandson of Kris Jenner & Robert Kardashian and step-grandson of Caitlyn Jenner. Likewise, his great grandparents are Arthur Kardashian and Helen Kardashian.

Mason Dash Disick's Parents

Mason Dash Disick is the son of one of the most famous celebs of the entertainment world, Kourtney Kardashian and her ex-hubby, Scott Disick. Disick's parents were widely-acknowledged for their on/off the relationship.

Mason Dash Disick and his parents (©: Pinterest)

They first met in 2006 and initiated their relationship, and both of them became one of the prominent parts of Keeping Up With The Kardashians. They separated three times throughout their relationship in 2008, 2010, and 2015.

They welcomed Mason Dash Disick on 14 December 2009 after three years of dating. Similarly, Mason got two other siblings Penelope Scotland Disick and Reign Aston Disick, in 2012 and 2014, respectively.

Mason Dash Disick's Net Worth

Mason Dash Disick might be one of the wealthiest personalities of his age group. He may be ten but has already amassed the net worth of $500 thousand on his account, according to celebritynetworth.com.

Well, Mason is a reality star since before his birth, so there are no questions on how he had made such huge fortunes at such tender age.

As a reality star, he has appeared in Keeping Up with the Kardashians, as well its subsequent spin-offs, Kourtney and Khloe Take Miami, Kourtney, and Kim Take New York, and Khloe and Lamar.
