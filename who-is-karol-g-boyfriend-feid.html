Who Is Feid? - Meet Karol G's Boyfriend

Musical artist Karol G and Feid went public with their relationship this summer after being seen together for months out and about on what sure looked like romantic dates. The couple have worked on music together and frequently performed, but it wasn’t until June that they were photographed in Miami holding hands at the Kaseya Center. In September 2023, they were seen again holding hands in New York City.

Here’s everything to know about Karol G’s boyfriend Feid.

Who is musical artist Feid?

The musician’s birth name is Salomón Villada Hoyos, though he goes by Feid as a stage name that came from the phrase “Faith in God and in himself.”

Feid was born in Medellín, Colombia, in 1992, the son of a university professor and a psychologist. He eventually took music courses at the University of Antioquia and began focusing on learning to play a number of instruments, including the piano and guitar. He was particularly in love with reggaeton music.

He began as a songwriter, penning “Ginza,” and “Sigo Extrañándote” for J Balvin. He has also collaborated with Maluma, Nicky Jam, and Sebastian Yatra. His first solo album Morena came out in 2016.

Fied told El Tiempo in an interview, “Going from being a composer to being an artist is something different, I do it with more passion and love. Music is really what moves me every day to get up, to go to bed, to have lunch; in everything.”

His next album Feliz Cumpleaños Ferxxo got him into the top 10 for Billboard’s Top Latin Albums chart by its second week. Soon after, he started a two-week tour in the U.S. that sold out in 24 hours, and then made history by selling out two dates at the Puerto Rico coliseum in an hour and a half.

How long have Feid and Karol G been together?

The pair released a song title “Friki” in 2021, which sparked dating rumors, but they have been friends for a number of years. The video for the song was pretty sexy, though:

They initially denied rumors of anything more than friendship. In May 2022, Feid told a reporter, “If I had something to say, I’d let everyone know, but I don’t have anything to share...We made a song, I was with her on tour, I love her a lot and everything, but there is nothing beyond that.”

However, as early as March 2023, fans interpreted some of Karol G’s behavior on stage as a confirmation they were in a relationship. At the Estadio Hiram Bithorn in Puerto Rico, Feid performed “Friki” with his girlfriend, and then she made a surprise announcement. The star said that the love song “Tus Gafitas” on her album Mañana Será Bonito was written about Feid, announcing just before she sang it, “This song is for Feid!”

The song is about someone going through a heartbreaking end of a relationship and then meeting someone who encourages them to love again. Karol G had a pretty public breakup with her ex-boyfriend Anuel AA in 2021 after three years together. But she loves again.

They've been seen out together on a date as recently as Sept. 7, 2023.
