Biography

Mark Calderon was born in United States on September 27, 1970. Second tenor artist for the contemporary R&B band Color Me Badd who was one of three members to found the group. He and Bryan Abrams were the lone members of the group before they disbanded for the first time, and they remained the only members when they re-emerged as Color Me Badd in 2010. He worked with Stevie Brock on his Disney chart topping remake of the song “All 4 Love.” He was a friend of Color Me Badd co-founder Sam Watters in grade school.
