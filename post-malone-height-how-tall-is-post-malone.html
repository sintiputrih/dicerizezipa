Austin Richard Post was born July 4, 1995, in Syracuse, New York. He is professionally known as Post Malone, an American rapper, singer, and songwriter. His stage name was derived from inputting his birth name into a rap name generator.

Malone began playing the guitar in 2010 and auditioned for the American band Crown the Empire, but was turned down when his guitar strings broke during the audition.

Malone has hit tracks such as white inversion, congratulations, and goodbyes.
