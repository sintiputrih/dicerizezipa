Formula 1 2024 Season: Updates, Standings, and Major Stories

The 2024 season in Formula 1 has been off the charts with rivalries, unexpected wins, and the battle for the world's best driver and team. Into the heart of this thrilling season, we go, which means here are the latest updates so far.


The Unfolding Drama

Max Verstappen dominated the first 2024 season in Bahrain to open up the 2024 Formula 1 season, setting up a season that was captured in a series of thrilling races. Every Grand Prix from Bahrain to Monaco has seen its fair share of the unexpected, the jubilation, and the misfortune – keeping fans guessing.


Standings and Victories


Max Verstappen from Red Bull Racing leads the way in the current standings with a total of 169 points, with Charles Leclerc from Ferrari hot on his heels with 138 points, followed by Lando Norris from McLaren with 113 points It is a fierce fight for glory as these drivers go on to prove their rare talent and determination on the field.
Verstappen won the opening race of the season in Bahrain and then dominated in Saudi Arabia. Against that, a Melbourne shake-up had Carlos Sainz's Ferrari leading the way before Verstappen then re-found his stride with successive triumphs in Japan and China. That was followed by Lando Norris claiming his first Formula 1 race in Miami before Charles Leclerc won his home race in Monaco to close the gap at the top of the drivers' standings.
