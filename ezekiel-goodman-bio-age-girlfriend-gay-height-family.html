Ezekiel Goodman Bio, Age, Girlfriend, Gay, Height, Family

If you ever thought Jennifer Love Hewitt and Freddie Prinze Jr.’s I Know What You Did Last Summer would be the only series on the I Know What You Did Last Summer franchise, you are certainly mistaken. Together, Amazon Studios and Sony Pictures Television Studios worked to produce the YA horror television series, I Know What You Did Last Summer, for Amazon Prime Video.

The first 4 episodes of the series aired with the show’s premiere on October 15, 2021, to mixed reception and the rest episodes debuting every week. A new cast of teenagers and adults, including Madison Iseman and Ezekiel Goodman, is at the center of the series.

In this article, we will talk about Ezekiel Goodman’s role in I Know What You Did Last Summer. We will also about his age, family, net worth, and girlfriend.

Ezekiel Goodman On “I Know What You Did Last Summer”

A modern take on the hit 1997 film adaptation, I Know What You Did Last Summer follows a group of friends who accidentally hit a pedestrian with their car during their graduation night. Sometime later, all of the teens involved in a car accident began getting threatening messages from someone who claims to have known all the things the teens did to cover up the accident. The real drama begins there.

Madison Iseman (plays Allison Grant and Lennon Grant), Bill Heck (plays Bruce Grant, Allison and Lennon’s father), Brianne Tju (plays Margot), Ashley Moore (plays Riley, Lennon’s friend), Sebastian Amoruso (plays Johnny Lagela), Fiona Rene (plays Lyla Kalei), Cassie Beck (plays Courtney), Brooke Bloom (plays Clara), Spencer Sutherland (plays Dale), Chrissie Fit (plays Kelly Craft), Sonya Balmores (Mil Gilbert) are the cast of the Amazon Prime Video’s show alongside Ezekiel Goodman.

Ezekiel Goodman plays the role of Dylan Scanlon. He is the love interest of Allison Grant and the best friend of Riley. Ezekiel was part of everything that happened on graduation night.

Ezekiel Goodman Net Worth

Since Ezekiel Goodman is very new to the mainstream media, his net worth is considerably lower. As of October 2021, his net worth was reported to be under $100 thousand.

Relatively a new name in the acting world, Ezekiel does not have many acting credits to his name. Dylan Scanlon in “I Know What You Did Last Summer” would be Ezekiel’s first mainstream role. He previously starred in short movies like Drive-In Date and A Shore.

Furthermore, Ezekiel wrote and starred in the mini-series, Rat Bastard. His latest short film “Peacocking” premiered at Utah’s Davey Fest. Ezekiel was credited as the actor, writer, and producer of that short movie.

As for his education, Ezekiel completed his Bachelor’s studies in Arts from Johns Hopkins University. Recently, he was pursuing an MFA Acting Program at the University of Southern California School of Dramatic Arts.

Furthermore, Ezekiel was also a member of the iO Theater and The Annoyance Theater in Chicago. I Know What You Did Last Summer star is represented by Lighthouse Management and Media and Eric Books at Goodman Genow.

Is Ezekiel Goodman Gay?

Coming to his sexuality, many people might be wondering if Ezekiel Goodman is straight or gay. Just to clear things out first, no Ezekiel is not gay.

Ezekiel Goodman Family

To talk about Ezekiel’s family, he is the only child of his mom, Sara Nemeth Goodman, and his dad, Nathaniel Goodman.

Ezekiel’s mom, Sara, is a producer and a writer best known for her works in I Know What You Did Last Summer, Preacher, and Gossip Girl. Ezekiel’s dad is a director, writer, and Director of Photography, popular for his works in Made for Love, Swamp Thing, Heroes, Falling Skies, Bitten, and Formosa Betrayed.

Gayle Baigelman is Ezekiel’s step-mom. She is an Operations Manager at Los Angeles Homeless Services Authority and Board Chair at Lipstick Angels in Los Angeles, California.

As for his siblings, Ezekiel has a step-sister and her name is Willa Baigelman. Willa works at Book Soup.
