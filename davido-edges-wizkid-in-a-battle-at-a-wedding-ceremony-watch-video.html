Davido Edges Wizkid In A Battle At Wedding Ceremony- Watch Video

There was fun galore at a recently held wedding somewhere in Nigeria when the MC for the August occasion tried to merge Wizkid and Davido’s fans in a beautiful charade.

The game demanded that the wedding guests danced and move in the direction of ‘Davido’- an inscription was being held by the groom while those who love Wizkid will dance and move towards the name ‘Wizzy’ which was being held up by the bride.

In the video, the Disc Jockey first played Wizkid’s ”In my bed” and the guests were playing the dancing game with little enthusiasm.

However, as Davido’s Dami Duro was played, the whole place went agog and swung into a different tempo which shows the massive love they have for Davido.
