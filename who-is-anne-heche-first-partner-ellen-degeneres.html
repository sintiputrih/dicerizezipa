Who is Anne Heche first partner Ellen DeGeneres?

Anne Heche is an American actress and director who is best known for portraying Vicky Hudson and Marley Love in the soap opera Another World.

During her career, she has been awarded many accolades including a Daytime Emmy Award, two Soap Opera Digest Awards, and a Tony Award nomination.

Who is Anne Heche first partner Ellen DeGeneres?

Prior to her two marriages, Heche was in a relationship with Ellen DeGeneres. She is an American comedian, television host, actress, writer, and producer.

From 2003 to 2022, she hosted the syndicated television talk show, The Ellen DeGeneres Show which earned her 33 Daytime Emmy Awards.

In 1997, DeGeneres came out as a lesbian, in the same year she began a relationship with Heche. The relationship lasted until 2000, with the pair saying that they would get a civil union if such became legal in Vermont.

Following their breakup, DeGeneres maintained a close affair with photographer Alexandra Hedison from 2000 to 2004. She is currently married to Australian-American actress, Portia de Rossi.

They got married in August 2008, at their home in Beverly Hills, California, where they currently live with their four dogs and three cats.
