Lilie James Was Found Murdered

A shocking tragedy unfolded at a prestigious Sydney school, where a 21-year-old female water polo instructor, Lilie James, met a gruesome end, brutally attacked with a hammer. The authorities are currently on a relentless manhunt for her alleged assailant.

Lilie’s life was tragically cut short when her lifeless body, bearing harrowing head injuries, was discovered inside St Andrew’s Cathedral School in the heart of the city’s CBD, right around midnight on a fateful Thursday morning.

The grim discovery came to light after her father, gripped by worry and fear, reached out to the police when Lilie failed to return home. The prime suspect in this chilling case is none other than her former colleague, 24-year-old Paul Thijssen.

Paul, who had once served as a sports coach at the prestigious private school, hailed from the Netherlands but moved to Australia in 2015 and attended St Andrew’s for years 10-12. What adds to the intrigue is the surveillance footage that captured Thijssen accompanying Ms. James into a school gym bathroom at approximately 7 pm on a Wednesday evening.

However, the shocking turn of events takes a darker twist as the footage shows Thijssen exiting the area well over an hour later, while Lilie James tragically never reemerges from that bathroom. It was around four hours later that Thijssen himself contacted the authorities.

Police were able to trace the chilling call to the ‘The Gap’ clifftop at Diamond Bay Reserve in Vaucluse, where they discovered a backpack and some of his belongings. Notably, Paul Thijssen had spent 18 months alone in Australia during the COVID-19 pandemic, a period that undoubtedly took a toll on many.

A source from the school reveals that while Thijssen may not have displayed signs of psychosis, this dark chapter in his life may have deeply affected him.

Adding another layer of complexity to this grim narrative, it has come to light that Lilie James and Paul Thijssen were in a ‘secret relationship’ for a few weeks leading up to her untimely demise. A close friend of Lilie’s disclosed that this ill-fated relationship had only recently come to an end, further shrouding this tragedy in mystery and sorrow.

Lilie James Parents and Siblings

On October 30, 2001, Lilie James (age 21) was born to her father, Jamie James, and mother, Peta James, in New South Wales, Australia. Sources revealed that Lilie grew up in Sydney and had a wonderful childhood.

Her parents fulfilled all her dreams. The James family used to go on frequent trips to exotic destinations including Hawaii. She was very good at sports and was a highly decorated athlete during her school days.

In 2019 she completed her schooling and joined the University of Technology Sydney. At UTS she graduated with a Bachelor’s degree in Sports Business in 2023. She was working as a Water Polo Instructor a the renowned St Andrew’s Cathedral School when she was murdered.
