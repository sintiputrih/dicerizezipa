Beyonc Twins Names Revealed - Beyonc and Jay-Z Newborn Twins' Names

Beyoncé and JAY-Z's twins are named Rumi and Sir, according to TMZ.

The tabloid reports that the company that owns the trademarks to Bey and Jay's names also just "filed legal docs to secure the rights" to the names Rumi Carter and Sir Carter.

The news arrives a week after rumors first claimed the twins were named Shawn and Bea, after their parents. As of right now, neither parent (or anyone from the family at all) has confirmed what the twins' names actually are yet.

The newborns, reportedly born on the week of June 12 at UCLA Medical Center, are new siblings for first-time big sister Blue Ivy, who is five years old.

Beyoncé first announced she was expecting twins in February with an epic photo that soon became the most-liked Instagram picture ever. We can't wait for her to populate our feeds with more epic photos of her two new little ones.
