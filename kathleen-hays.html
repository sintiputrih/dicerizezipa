Kathleen Hays – Career

Kathleen Hays is a university-trained economist. She has experience at the Federal Reserve and is now an on-air financial reporter for Bloomberg Television. This personality was formerly a reporter for Investor’s Business Daily, CNBC’s Squawk Box, and various CNN programming before joining Bloomberg.

This personality has spanned over 30 years of reporting on the US economy and the Federal Reserve. Hays has extended her coverage over the past 2 years to include Asia’s economies and Central Banks and frequently traveled to Japan.

Hays is an NYU money marketeer missionary former Governor and a member of the New York Economic Club. Moreover, after years as an on-air and internet economist reporter at CNBC and CNN, she joined Bloomberg in the year 2006. And, she worked on various programs as an interviewer, correspondent, and comments editor.

However, this personality began her career as Head of the New York office of the financial news service Markets News International. Likewise, she joined Reuters, then worked with Investor’s Business Day as an economic reporter and New York Office Chief.
