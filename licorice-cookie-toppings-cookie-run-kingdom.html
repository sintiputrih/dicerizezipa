Licorice Cookie Toppings - Cookie Run Kingdom

Cookie Run Kingdom Licorice Cookie toppings should play to the strengths of the cookie, as is the case with all cookies that you’re applying toppings to. Given the role that Licorice cookie has, one particular build definitely comes out on top, but there’s always room for experimentation. In our Licorice Cookie Toppings – Cookie Run Kingdom guide, we’ll explain what we think is the best build for this particular character and why.

Toppings for Licorice Cookie in Cookie Run Kingdom

The best toppings for the Licorice Cookie in Cookie Run Kingdom are five Swift Chocolate. Why? Well, let’s consider what Licorice Cookie actually does. For one, it’s a middle cookie, meaning that he stands around the center of the party. More importantly, however, he’s a Magic cookie, meaning that his skill is his most powerful tool. In fact, his skill summons both Black Lightning and Licorice Servants, aka he summons more troops. On top of that, the spell also boosts the entire party’s Defense for a brief period.
