Wee Man Jackass Oompa-Loompa

Jason Acuña is widely popular for starring in the reality comedy TV series Jackass. Wee plays himself in Jackassin 16 episodes which aired between 2000 and 2002. Furthermore, some of his pranks in Jackass involve skating as an Oompa-Loompa, kicking himself in the head, and doing deep-knee bends while holding basketball star Shaquille O’Neal on his back.

Besides the Jackass series, he has appeared in Jackass: The Movie (2002), Jackass Number Two (2006), Jackass 2.5 (2007), Jackass 3D (2010), Jackass 3.5 (2011), and Jackassworld.com: 24 Hour Takeover (2008). He is also the voice actor in Jackass: The Game (2007).

He also appeared in the series Armed and Famous in 2007 and served as host for Scarred Live in July 2007.

Wee Man Chronic Tacos

Besides comedy and entertainment, Jason Acuña is also a successful businessman and notable investor in the Chronic Tacos chain of fast-casual Mexican restaurants. He opened his first franchise in Redondo Beach, CA in 2010 but closed it after low returns from the shop. Nevertheless, he opened another Chronic Tacos franchise in Long Beach, CA in Feb 2018.

Additionally, Wee served as a subscription manager for the skateboard magazine Big Brother.

Wee Man Van/ House

Wee sold his Hermosa Beach house for $2.3 million in 2018 and moved into his 4X4 Mercedes sprinter van in California. His van has stainless steel shower with toilette, electric stove, sink, jack knife bed, fridge, AC, and even a rope for a gym. He made the decision to move from his Hermosa Beach home after he realized ‘the more things you have the more issues you have thus he opted for a simpler life.
