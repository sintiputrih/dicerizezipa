Michele Weaver Bio (Wiki)

Who is Michele Weaver?

Michele Weaver is an actress and director. She is renowned as Nuri of Love Is and Faren of Illicit.

Last, in 2020, she appeared in the TV series, Council of Dads as Luly Perry.

Upcoming Projects

Next, she will be appearing in the TV series, Dance in Flight: The Film in the role of a dancer. Also, she will make her appearance in Beverly H. as Morgan.

As of now, both of her movie projects are at the verse of post-production.

What are the birth facts of Michele Weaver?

Michele Weaver celebrates her birthday on the 20th of March. Her birthplace is Littleton, Colorado, USA.

Her father, Steve Weaver is from Colorado, the United States whereas her mother, Nicole Weaver is of Haitian roots.

She has two brothers, Jean-Paul Weaver and Luuk Weaver. They are of British-Germanic-Haitian ethnicity.

Michele’s Education

She joined Pepperdine University in Malibu, California.

Michele Weaver- Professional Life, Career

After graduating from the University, Michele Weaver started her professional career as a theater artist. Moving forwards, she started making small appearances in short movies and commercials.

In 2011, she made her first appearance in the short movie, One Night at Monich’s. Following that, she appeared as Rebecca Copeland in short, Unidentified. The same year, she debuted in movies with Death Suspects a Murder in minor roles.

Later, she started featuring TV series in minor roles. However, she got recognized after the short, Diana Leigh as a lead character. Later, she was featured in the musical civil drama, Daughter of Eve.

After those shorts, she resumed her career in movies. Some of her movies include 2015- Sister Code, 2016- Illicit, and 2019- Nighthawks.
