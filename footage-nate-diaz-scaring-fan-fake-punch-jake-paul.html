Nate Diaz went viral when he attended Jake Paul's rematch win over Tyron Woodley in 2021.

The former UFC star was at the event to support his friend and teammate Chris Avila, who beat Anthony Taylor on the undercard.

On the day before the fight, Diaz was involved in an altercation at the weigh-in.

And he found drama once again on fight night itself.

A video emerged showing Nate throwing a fake punch at a man, causing him to hurl his drink in the air out of fright.

It was unclear if the man said something to provoke him.
