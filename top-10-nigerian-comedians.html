Top 10 Nigerian Comedians

These comedy maestros have become synonymous with side-splitting humour, enchanting audiences not just in Nigeria but across the globe. With their distinct comedic styles, they have etched their names in the annals of Nigerian entertainment, leaving an indelible mark on the hearts and funny bones of people of all ages.

From the uproarious performances of Basketmouth to the infectious wit of Ayo Makun, these comedians have ascended to the status of household names, amassing a legion of followers on social media. Their comedy sketches, stand-up specials, and silver-screen ventures have captured the attention of Nigerians, making them an integral part of the entertainment landscape. Their comedic prowess knows no bounds, enchanting audiences with their quick wit, impeccable timing, and astute observations.

Basketmouth and AY have become beacons of inspiration in the comedy realm, pushing the boundaries of their craft and redefining the art of making people laugh. Their unique perspectives and ability to connect with diverse audiences have earned them critical acclaim and the adoration of fans worldwide.

These comedians have not only brought laughter but have also contributed to the growth and development of the Nigerian comedy scene. Their creative endeavours have paved the way for emerging talents, inspiring a new generation of comedians to follow their passion and share their unique voices with the world.

10. Lasisi Elenu

Lasisi Elenu, the comedic virtuoso hailing from Nigeria, graces the stage with his uproarious presence and infectious laughter. With a unique comedic style, he effortlessly navigates the intricacies of his love life, weaving tales that tickle the funny bone and warm the heart.

His comedic prowess has garnered him an array of accolades, including the illustrious Best Male Comedian title at the esteemed 2018 City People Entertainment Awards and the highly coveted 2019 Future Awards Africa Nigeria Prize for Comedy. These esteemed honours bear witness to his remarkable talent and the indelible mark he has made on the comedy landscape.

Lasisi Elenu reigns supreme as one of Nigeria’s most revered jesters, with an uncanny ability to captivate audiences and leave them in stitches. Whether he takes to the stage or the digital sphere, his magnetic presence shines through, leaving a lasting impression on all who enjoy experiencing his comedic genius.

Through the power of social media, Lasisi Elenu has embraced the digital age, sharing his comedic sketches and engaging with his devoted following. His online persona radiates authenticity and charm, inviting fans into his world of laughter and mirth.

9. Mark Angel

Mark Angel, a renowned Nigerian comedian, has carved a special place for himself in the hearts of audiences with his side-splitting comedy skits. Collaborating with the remarkable child actress Emmanuella Samuel, they have garnered an immense following on social media and garnered numerous accolades. 

Mark Angel‘s comedy is cherished for its simplicity and relatability, appealing to both young and old alike. Beyond laughter, he engages in philanthropic endeavours, utilizing his platform to raise awareness and support various causes. His contributions to the Nigerian entertainment industry have been recognized through prestigious awards, including the 2017 NEA Award for Best Comedy Act and the 2019 City People Entertainment Award for Best Comedy Skit.

8. Sabinus (Mr Funny)

Sabinus, better known as Mr Funny, is a Nigerian comedian who has captivated audiences with his uproarious stand-up comedy and skits. He has become a household name in the Nigerian entertainment scene, spreading smiles and laughter among people of all ages. 

What sets Sabinus apart is his distinct sense of humour, which leaves a lasting impression on anyone who has the pleasure of experiencing his performances. But let’s not forget that he is just one of the many talented comedians making waves in Nigeria’s vibrant entertainment industry. 

From the legendary Basketmouth to the hilarious Ayo Makun, these comedians bring unique approaches and styles, ensuring an abundance of laughter and entertainment for all.

7. I Go Dye

I Go Dye, hailing from Nigeria, is a multi-talented individual. He is known for his exceptional comedic skills, acting abilities, and musical talents. His unique approach to comedy sets him apart, which delves into social commentary, shedding light on pressing issues like corruption and poverty in Nigeria. 

I Go Dye‘s comedic genius has not gone unnoticed, as he has received numerous prestigious awards, including Best Comedian at the 2010 Nigeria Entertainment Awards and Best Male Comedian at the 2011 City People Entertainment Awards. 

Alongside celebrated comedians like Basketmouth and Ayo Makun, I Go Dye captivates audiences with his quick wit and astute observations. His performances are a delightful blend of humour and insight, showcasing his ability to entertain while provoking thought.

6. Akpororo

Akpororo, the dynamic and multi-talented comedian, actor, and singer, hails from the vibrant Nigerian entertainment scene. With his boundless energy and captivating comedic style, he has carved a niche for himself as a true maestro of laughter. Renowned for his electric performances, Akpororo has garnered prestigious awards, including the coveted titles of Best Comedian at the Nigeria Entertainment Awards in 2013 and Best Male Comedian at the City People Entertainment Awards in 2014.

Akpororo‘s comedic finesse knows no bounds, as he effortlessly engages audiences with his magnetic stage presence and uproarious humour. His ability to weave laughter into every moment is a testament to his innate talent and dedication to his craft. With each performance, he leaves a lasting impression on both young and old, enchanting them with his unique blend of wit and charm.

In addition to his comedic genius, Akpororo is a versatile entertainer who has ventured into acting and music. His on-screen performances showcase his depth and versatility as an actor, while his melodic voice serenades audiences, revealing another facet of his artistic prowess. Through his passion and creativity, he has become a beacon of inspiration for aspiring artists in Nigeria and beyond.

5. Julius Agwu

Julius Agwu, the comedic maestro hailing from Nigeria, is a true virtuoso of laughter. As a comedian, actor, singer, and songwriter, he deftly combines his talents to create a tapestry of humour and entertainment. His unique brand of observant comedy delves into the intricacies of Nigerian life, leaving audiences in stitches.

Julius Agwu‘s comedic brilliance has garnered numerous accolades, including the prestigious Best Comedian award at the 2006 Nigeria Entertainment Awards and the 2007 City People Entertainment Awards. These honours testify to his remarkable ability to connect with his audience and elicit uproarious laughter.

Beyond the laughter, Julius Agwu‘s comedic prowess is laced with thought-provoking social commentary. Through his witty observations, he sheds light on Nigeria’s pressing issues, sparking conversations and offering insight. His insightful humour has earned him the admiration and respect of his peers and audiences.

With his infectious charm and magnetic stage presence, Julius Agwu has carved a special place in the hearts of his fans. His performances invite him to join a comedic journey, where laughter becomes a form of catharsis and unity. His impact extends beyond the stage as he inspires and uplifts through his artistic endeavours.

4. Broda Shaggi

Broda Shaggi, the comedic virtuoso hailing from Nigeria, has captivated audiences with unparalleled wit and charm. A true multifaceted talent, he seamlessly weaves the roles of a comedian, actor, and social media influencer into a tapestry of laughter and amusement.

In recognition of his comedic brilliance, Broda Shaggi has been awarded prestigious accolades. The title of Best Male Comedian at the City People Entertainment Awards in 2019 and the illustrious Future Awards Africa Nigeria Prize for Comedy in 2020 adorn his illustrious career. These accolades are a testament to his ability to evoke laughter and joy from the hearts of his audience.

What sets Broda Shaggi apart is his unique comedic style, which revolves around the sacred bond between a mother and her son. Unlike his fellow comedic stars like Akpororo and Julius Agwu, Broda Shaggi‘s humour strikes a chord with audiences by tapping into the relatable dynamics of family relationships. With each uproarious performance, he elicits laughter that resonates deep within the souls of his viewers.

3. Ayo Makun

Ayo Makun, a multitalented force in the Nigerian entertainment industry, is a comedian, actor, producer, and television host who leaves an indelible mark on the hearts of audiences. With his acclaimed comedy extravaganza, AY Live, Ayo has carved a niche for himself, showcasing his unique humour and captivating the masses.

Amidst a constellation of prestigious awards, including the 2014 Best Comedian at the Nigeria Entertainment Awards and the 2015 Best Male Comedian at the City People Entertainment Awards, Ayo Makun‘s comedic prowess shines through. His ability to effortlessly weave laughter into the fabric of his performances has earned him well-deserved recognition and the adoration of fans worldwide.

Under the stage name AY, Ayo Makun unleashes a whirlwind of wit and charm, captivating audiences with his quick-thinking improvisations and infectious energy. Through his acclaimed show AY Live, he curates an unforgettable experience, inviting viewers into a world of laughter and joy.

2. Basketmouth

Basketmouth, a remarkable comedian, actor, and rapper from Nigeria, is renowned for his quick wit and ability to find humour in the most unexpected places. With a string of accolades, including the prestigious 2009 Nigeria Entertainment Award for Best Comedian and the esteemed 2010 City People Entertainment Award for Best Male Comedian, Basketmouth has solidified his position as a comedic powerhouse.

His comedic prowess lies in his unique ability to extract laughter from the mundane aspects of everyday life, inviting audiences to join him on a journey of self-reflection and lighthearted amusement. Basketmouth‘s charm and stage presence undeniably captivate audiences and create an unforgettable experience long after the curtains close.

Beyond his comedic exploits, Basketmouth‘s lyrical dexterity and rhythmic finesse have earned him recognition in the rap world. His words flow effortlessly, painting vivid pictures and evoking emotions with every line. Whether delivering a humorous verse or delving into more profound themes, his command of language leaves a lasting impact.

As an actor, Basketmouth showcases his versatility and range, seamlessly transitioning between comedic and dramatic roles. He brings characters to life with authenticity and depth, captivating audiences with his nuanced performances. His magnetic presence on screen captivates viewers and leaves them wanting more.

1. Bovi Ugboma

Bovi, the comedy maestro, is a Nigerian star renowned for his razor-sharp wit and magnetic stage presence. With a knack for observational humour that delves into the intricate dynamics of family and relationships, Bovi has carved a niche for himself in the comedy sphere. His ability to bring laughter to the masses has earned him prestigious accolades, including the esteemed Best Comedian award at the 2012 Nigeria Entertainment Awards and the coveted Best Male Comedian honour at the 2013 City People Entertainment Awards.

Bovi‘s independent venture, AY Live, is a testament to his creative genius. Through this self-crafted platform, he masterfully weaves hilarious tales that strike a chord with audiences, effortlessly tapping into the shared experiences that make us all human. His magnetic charisma and quick-witted banter forge an unbreakable connection with his fans, leaving them roaring with laughter and begging for more.

Beyond the comedy stage, Bovi‘s talents extend to the silver screen and the written word. His acting prowess brings characters to life, infusing them with depth and authenticity, while his written works captivate readers with their clever insights and thought-provoking narratives. Bovi‘s versatility as an artist knows no bounds, allowing him to leave an indelible mark on the entertainment industry.
