Actress Lori Loughlin Booted From 90210

Say goodbye to Lori Loughlin.

The 46-year-old actress, who plays Tristan Wilds' and Shenae Grimes' mom Debbie Wilson on 90210, will be leaving the CW drama at the end of its third season.

"I have enjoyed the past few years working on 90210 and adore all of the cast and crew, as well as the team at the CW and CBS TV Studios," Loughlin recently told E! News. "I am grateful for having had the opportunity to be a part of this wonderful show."

But the Full House alum isn't the only one leaving the famous zip-code later this spring: Ryan Eggold, who plays high school teacher Ryan Matthews, is also reportedly leaving the teen soap.
