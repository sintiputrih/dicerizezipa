Who is Dave Asprey:

Dave Asprey is a famous Entrepreneur. He was born on October 30, 1973 and his birthplace is Canada. Dave is also well known as, Entrepreneur and author who is known for his founding of the Bulletproof brand of health and wellness. In 2009, he founded the upgraded coffee brand Bulletproof Coffee, which led to his publishing of the book The Bulletproof Diet in December 2014.

Dave is originated from Canada. His preferred clients include the actor Jeremy Piven, who wrote a testimonial for the publication of his book, The Bulletproof Diet.
