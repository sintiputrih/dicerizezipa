Who is Jessica Alba?

Jessica Alba is an American actress, model, and businesswoman.

She is immensely eminent for being an actress in the hit television show ‘Dark Angel’ whereby, she portrayed the role of super-soldier, Max Guevara.

Also, Jessica Alba is known for her starring in some immensely popular films like ‘Fantastic Four’ (2005), ‘Into the Blue’ (2005), and ‘Sin City (2005).

Age, Parents, Siblings, Family, Ethnicity, Nationality

Jessica Alba was in Pomona, California, United States of America. She was born on April 28, 1981.

Her nationality is American and her ethnicity is Mixed (Danish, Welsh, German, English, French, Mexican, and Canadian).

Alba was born to Catherine Louisa (mother) and Mark David Alba (father). She has a younger brother named Joshua Alba.

Jessica Alba was raised in Biloxi, Mississippi, and Del Rio, Texas before returning back to Claremont, California at the age of nine.

Jessica Alba was raised in a very conservative family, a traditional, Catholic, Latin American family along with her younger sister Joshua Alba.

Jessica Alba: Professional Life and Career

Jessica Alba initiated her career with her participation in some competitions which led her to work as an actress in her debut feature film ‘Camp Nowhere’ in 1994 whereby, she played the role of Gail which was followed by some other films along with her debut television show ‘The Secret World of Alex Mack’ in which she played the role of Jessica.

In 2000, she got her breakthrough with an opportunity to play in the Fox sci-fi television series Dark Angel’ and won many awards including Teen Choice Award for “Choice Actress” and Saturn Award for Best Actress along with a Golden Globe Award nomination.

After her immense success, she played in some well-known movies like ‘Fantastic Four (2005), ‘Into the Blue’ (2005), and ‘And Sin City (2005).

Also, Jessica Alba was #1 on Maxim’s Hot 100 Babe List during the year 2001 along with her selection as the fifth Sexiest Female Star for 2002 in a Hollywood.com poll.

Jessica Alba has also hosted several awards shows efficiently and in 2010 acted in 5 films.

She ventured into business in 2012  and along with her business partner Christopher Gavigan launched ‘The Honest Company which deals in household goods, diapers, and body care products.

Jessica Alba owns 15-20% shares in the company. She has also penned a book called ‘The Honest Life’ which became NY’s best seller.

In 2015, she launched her skincare and beauty products called Honest Beauty.
